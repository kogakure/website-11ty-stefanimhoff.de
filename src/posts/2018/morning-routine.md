## My Morning Routine

- **Wake up** at 7:00 AM
- **Read** in bed for an hour
- **Get up**, make my bed, move, and do some stretches
- Open the windows everywhere to get **fresh air** in
- Record weight
- **Drink** something
- **Learn** languages for 15 minutes
- **Workout** with Calisthenics for 30-45 minutes
- Bathroom & Clothes
- **Meditate** for 15-20 minutes
- Prepare and eat **breakfast** while reading RSS Feeds
- **Prepare for the day**, by checking the **calendar** and select **To-Do** items for the day
- Get Ready

I do this routine every day, the only difference is, that I do not use an alarm clock at the weekend, but instead wake up when I wake up, which is usually around 7:00 AM. Iâ€™m doing it now for a month and Iâ€™m really happy with it. Iâ€™ll keep updating this post, should my routine change.
